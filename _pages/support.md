Saikei Civil is developed by Desert Springs Civil Engineering PLLC as part of our commitment to open-source infrastructure design tools. Your support directly funds continued development.

---

## Why Support Matters

Saikei Civil receives dedicated professional engineering attention. This means:

- **Faster development cycles** — dedicated focus on the roadmap
- **Professional-grade code** — engineering rigor applied to every feature
- **Responsive maintenance** — bugs and issues addressed promptly
- **Long-term stability** — sustainable development, not sporadic updates

Funding ensures this work continues and accelerates the path to production-ready infrastructure design tools accessible to everyone.

---

## What Your Support Funds

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Development** | Full-time engineering work on core features |
| **Infrastructure** | Hosting, CI/CD, testing environments |
| **Documentation** | Tutorials, guides, and reference materials |
| **Community** | Issue triage, pull request reviews, user support |
| **Standards Work** | IFC 4.3 compliance testing and buildingSMART engagement |
